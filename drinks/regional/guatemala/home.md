# Drinks of Guatemala

![Drinks of Guatemala](resources/hero.jpg)

The atol carts of Antigua's market pour mugs of warm white-corn atol at sunrise, ladled from clay pots and dusted with toasted pumpkin seed. The cacao tradition runs deep here, where the Maya first ground the bean: tiste blends toasted maize with cacao and cinnamon into a cold spiced drink. Rosa de Jamaica, hibiscus steeped with ginger and panela, fills jugs at every comedor; coffee from Huehuetenango and Antigua finishes the table.
