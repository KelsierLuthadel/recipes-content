# Drinks of Hungary

![Drinks of Hungary](resources/hero.jpg)

Fröccs, the wine spritzer with its codified named ratios (kisfröccs at lunch, nagyfröccs in the early evening, hosszúlépés for the long afternoon). Tokaji aszú after dessert, szörp (homemade fruit cordials) at every aunt's kitchen, Unicum after a heavy meal.
