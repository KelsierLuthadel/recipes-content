# Drinks of New Orleans

![Drinks of New Orleans](resources/hero.jpg)

The cocktails of the French Quarter: café brûlot flamed tableside at Antoine's, the Vieux Carré at the Hotel Monteleone's Carousel Bar, absinthe frappés at the Old Absinthe House, café au lait with chicory at Café du Monde at three in the morning. Theatrical, brown-spirit-heavy, mostly invented before 1900.
