# Drinks of San Marino

![Drinks of San Marino](resources/hero.jpg)

The Republic's tiny mountain vineyards turn out Sangiovese as the table-wine backbone of every San Marinese dinner, the bottles labelled by the Consorzio Vini Tipici di San Marino. The after-meal pour is the caffè corretto, espresso laced with grappa, shared with Romagna across the border but distinct in the small-batch San Marinese grappa it leans on. Up in the lower villages on the western slope, tilia (lime-flower tea, picked when the trees bloom in late spring) is the calming cup of the evening, served unsweetened in a glass.
