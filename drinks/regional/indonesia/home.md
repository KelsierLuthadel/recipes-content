# Drinks of Indonesia

![Drinks of Indonesia](resources/hero.jpg)

Es teh manis (sweet iced tea) on every warung table; jamu, the herbal-medicinal turmeric-tamarind tonics sold from the ibu jamu's basket; kopi tubruk where the grounds settle at the bottom of the glass; wedang jahe and bandrek for the cold; sekoteng in the rain.
