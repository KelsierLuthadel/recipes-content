# Drinks of Israel

![Drinks of Israel](resources/hero.jpg)

Café hafuch ("upside-down coffee") at every Tel Aviv breakfast - milk poured first, espresso layered on top in a tall glass; limonana, the frozen lemon-mint slush invented in a 1990s ad campaign and now ubiquitous across the Levant; sahlab in winter, te nana (mint tea) at every meal.
