# Danish Drinks

![Danish Drinks](resources/hero.jpg)

Denmark's drinking culture has four cornerstones: the snaps-and-beer ritual at every Danish meal involving cured fish (akvavit alongside pilsner, with snapsvisor sung between rounds); the Christmas season's gløgg (the Danish answer to mulled wine, sharper and more cherry-forward than the Swedish glögg); a deep beer tradition centred on the two great Danish breweries (Carlsberg and Tuborg); the elderflower-cordial homemade tradition (hyldeblomstsaft, eaten with sparkling water all summer); and the traditional kaffepunch - strong black coffee with a generous dash of akvavit, the warming farmhouse afternoon ritual.
