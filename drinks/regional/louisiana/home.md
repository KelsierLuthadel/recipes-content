# Drinks of Louisiana

![Drinks of Louisiana](resources/hero.jpg)

The cocktails of New Orleans and the wider state: the Sazerac (Louisiana's official cocktail, rye whiskey rinsed with absinthe), the Hurricane (the Pat O'Brien's tropical rum punch born of a 1940s rum glut), the Ramos Gin Fizz (Henry Ramos's 1888 meringue-headed showpiece), the Vieux Carré at the Carousel Bar. Strong, citrus-bright, built for warm Southern nights.
