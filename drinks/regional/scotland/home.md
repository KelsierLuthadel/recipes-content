# Drinks of Scotland

![Drinks of Scotland](resources/hero.jpg)

The drinks of Scotland: single-malt and blended Scotch whisky (the world's most famous spirit, with five distinct regional styles - Highland, Lowland, Speyside, Islay, Campbeltown - each with its own character), Drambuie and Glayva (the herbal whisky liqueurs that finish a Scottish dinner), Atholl Brose (the ancient Highland oatmeal-honey-whisky infusion), and Irn-Bru (the bright orange caffeinated soft drink that's somehow Scotland's other national drink, outselling Coca-Cola in Scotland for decades).
