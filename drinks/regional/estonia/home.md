# Drinks of Estonia

![Drinks of Estonia](resources/hero.jpg)

Kali (the Estonian rye-bread kvass) pours from every summer kiosk, lightly fermented, faintly sour and barely alcoholic. Vana Tallinn, the rum-based Soviet-era state liqueur with notes of citrus peel, cinnamon and vanilla, has gone from a Tallinn bar fixture to an international export. Rose-petal syrups and homemade berry cordials handle the children's table; spring brings the old tradition of tapping birch trees for fresh sap. In Tallinn and Tartu a craft-beer revival now sits alongside the saaremaa farmhouse breweries that never stopped.
