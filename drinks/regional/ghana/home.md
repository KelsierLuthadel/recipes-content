# Drinks of Ghana

![Drinks of Ghana](resources/hero.jpg)

Sobolo at every road-side stand, the deep-red hibiscus drink steeped with ginger, clove and pineapple skin and sold ice-cold from chest-coolers. Asaana, the fermented-corn drink, slightly sweet-sour and ginger-laced, poured at funeral and wedding processions. Akpeteshie, the palm-wine-distilled local gin (sometimes called "kill-me-quick"), the everyday spirit. Fresh palm wine still plays its part at chiefly receptions, while Star Beer and Club Beer have risen as the bar standards alongside.
