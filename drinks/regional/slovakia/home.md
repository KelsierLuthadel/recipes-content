# Drinks of Slovakia

![Drinks of Slovakia](resources/hero.jpg)

Borovička and slivovica are the village pours of Slovakia: juniper spirit from the foothills, plum brandy from the home still, both drunk neat in a small thick-walled glass before lunch or after a long walk. Tatratea is the modern mountain-bar drink, a high-proof herbal-tea liqueur invented in the High Tatras and now poured in every ski-lodge in the country. Eastern Slovakia shares the Tokaj wine region with Hungary across the border, and the goral villages of the high country still run small-batch fruit stills under the old farmhouse tradition.
