# Drinks of Pakistan

![Drinks of Pakistan](resources/hero.jpg)

Kashmiri chai, the famously pink tea boiled long with baking soda and topped with crushed pistachios; the Lahori sweet lassi, thick and creamy in a tall metal tumbler with malai floating on top; doodh patti at the chai-wallah; Rooh Afza poured into cold milk in summer.
