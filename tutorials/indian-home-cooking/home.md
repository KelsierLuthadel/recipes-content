# Indian Home Cooking Course

## Overview

A foundations course on home-style Indian cooking - distinct from the existing [BIR (British-Indian-Restaurant) course](../bir-curry/home.md). Where BIR builds a curry from a pre-made base gravy in 8 minutes, home cooking starts with whole spices, builds the masala in the pan, and treats each dish as a self-contained one-pot. This course covers the masala system, the tarka (tempering) technique, dal mastery, rice and roti, and the regional traditions that change everything about how the food is built.
