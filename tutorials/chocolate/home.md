# Chocolate Work Course

## Overview

A chocolate course covering the bean-to-bar fundamentals, tempering by the classic and microwave methods, ganache ratios, truffle technique, moulded bars and bonbons, chocolate sauce and glazes, and the science of cocoa percentages, conching and crystal forms that determines whether a bar snaps cleanly or sits soft in your hand.
