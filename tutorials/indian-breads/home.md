# Indian Breads Course

## Overview

A deep dive into the breads of India - the everyday roti, the layered paratha (plain and stuffed), the leavened tandoor breads (naan and kulcha), the deep-fried puffs (puri and bhatura), and the techniques that make each work. Standalone from the [BIR course](../bir-curry/home.md) and the [Indian home cooking course](../indian-home-cooking/home.md), where breads are touched on briefly; this course gives each bread its own page.
