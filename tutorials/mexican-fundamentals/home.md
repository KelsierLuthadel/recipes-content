# Mexican Fundamentals Course

## Overview

A foundations course on home-style Mexican cooking. Masa nixtamalisation and tortilla pressing, the canonical salsas (red / green / cooked / cruda), beans from scratch, the seven moles of Oaxaca, and the compositional approach of the Mexican plate. Pairs with the existing [cuisine/mexican/](../../cuisine/mexican/) recipes.
