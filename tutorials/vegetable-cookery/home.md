# Vegetable Cookery Course

## Overview

A vegetable course covering roasting, blanching, braising, pickling and raw preparations. The science of why a roasted carrot tastes nothing like a boiled one, when to salt and when to blanch, the seasonal calendar that determines what is good to cook with this week, and how to think about a vegetable as a primary rather than a side.
