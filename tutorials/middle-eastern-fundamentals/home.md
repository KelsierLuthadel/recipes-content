# Middle Eastern Fundamentals Course

## Overview

A foundations course on Middle Eastern home cooking - the cuisine of Lebanon, Syria, Palestine, Jordan, Egypt, Iraq, and the wider Levant + Mashriq. Bread and grain, the mezze tradition (hummus, baba ghanoush, labneh, muhammara, fattoush, tabbouleh), kebabs (kofta vs shish), the spices (sumac, za'atar, baharat), and the regional distinctions between Lebanese, Syrian, Palestinian, Egyptian, Iraqi, and Persian. Pairs with [cuisine/lebanese/](../../cuisine/lebanese/), [cuisine/syria/](../../cuisine/syria/), [cuisine/persian/](../../cuisine/persian/), and the wider middle-east tree.
