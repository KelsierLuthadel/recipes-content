# Charcuterie & Curing Course

## Overview

A charcuterie course covering the salt-and-cure science, home dry-cured bacon, gravlax, duck confit and rillettes, whole-muscle dry-cured pork, fresh and dry-cured sausages, the cold-smoke versus hot-smoke distinction, and the food-safety rules that keep everything edible.
