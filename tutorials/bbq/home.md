# BBQ & Smoking Course

## Overview

A BBQ course covering the low-and-slow American tradition: brisket, ribs, pulled pork, the wood-and-fuel choices that flavour each, the rubs and mops and sauces that build the bark, the smoker formats from kettle barbecue through offset stick burner, and the science of collagen breakdown, smoke ring and stall that govern every long cook.
