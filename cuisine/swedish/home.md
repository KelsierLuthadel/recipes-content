# Swedish Cuisine

![Swedish Cuisine](resources/hero.jpg)

Sweden's husmanskost (home cooking) of meatballs, gravlax, herring in mustard sauce, pickled-cucumber salad and the sweet-savoury combinations that define Scandinavian cooking. Crispbread (knäckebröd), tunnbröd (thin flatbread), and the open-faced sandwich smörgåsbord tradition. The fika afternoon coffee-and-pastry break with cinnamon buns (kanelbullar) and cardamom cakes.
