# Serbian Cuisine

![Serbian Cuisine](resources/hero.jpg)

Serbian cuisine sits at the Balkan-Ottoman-Habsburg crossroads, drawing grilled meats and stuffed vegetables from the south, paprika and stews from the east, and breaded cutlets and pastries from the north. Ćevapi, sarma and pljeskavica anchor the meat tradition; kajmak (the soft clotted cream cheese) and ajvar (the autumn pepper relish) sit on every table; the rakija toast opens every meal and closes most of them.
