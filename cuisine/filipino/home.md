# Filipino Cuisine

![Filipino Cuisine](resources/hero.jpg)

A cuisine layered by Spanish, Chinese, American and Malay influences over its native base. Fish sauce, soy, vinegar and calamansi citrus drive the seasoning; coconut, garlic and tamarind anchor the bowl. Defining dishes include adobo (vinegar-soy braising), sinigang (sour soup), kare-kare (peanut stew) and pancit noodles, with rice at every meal and lechon at the centre of celebrations.
