# Tennessee Cuisine

![Tennessee Cuisine](resources/hero.jpg)

Tennessee cooking: the great American barbecue state plus the spice tradition Nashville hot chicken built. Memphis dry-rub ribs slow-smoked over hickory, Memphis-style pulled pork sandwiched with vinegar-tang slaw, the legendary Nashville hot chicken (cayenne-and-lard paste on fried chicken, served over white bread with pickles), Memphis brisket. Country-fried steak with white pepper gravy, fried catfish from the Tennessee River, country ham biscuits, fried bologna sandwiches and tennessee meatloaf anchor the everyday plate. Chess pie, peanut-butter fudge and Tennessee whiskey cake close the meal; skillet cornbread, baked beans and fried okra make up the canonical Tennessee plate alongside any smoked meat.
