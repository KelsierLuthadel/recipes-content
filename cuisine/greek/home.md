# Greek Cuisine

![Greek Cuisine](resources/hero.jpg)

Cooking shaped by olive oil, lemon, oregano and the Mediterranean larder. Feta, halloumi and yogurt anchor the dairy side; lamb, octopus and white-fish dominate the protein; chickpeas and pulses ground the rest. Defining techniques include slow-braised giouvetsi and stifado, charcoal-grilled souvlaki, layered moussaka and pastitsio, and the daily-bread role of phyllo wrapped around savoury and sweet fillings.
