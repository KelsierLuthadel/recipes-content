# Czech Cuisine

![Czech Cuisine](resources/hero.jpg)

Czechia's bread-and-pork tradition: pork roast (vepřo) with knedlíky bread dumplings and zelí sauerkraut, svíčková (sirloin in cream sauce), guláš stew, the lunchtime sausage in a tunneled roll (párek v rohlíku), open-faced obložené chlebíčky, and the bottomless pilsner tradition that gave the world Pilsner Urquell. The most beer-drinking country per capita on Earth.
