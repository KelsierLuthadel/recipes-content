# Fijian Cuisine

![Fijian Cuisine](resources/hero.jpg)

A Pacific cuisine built on coconut, taro (dalo), cassava and the sea, with a deep Indo-Fijian curry tradition layered on top from a century of cultural exchange. Earth-oven feasts (lovo), citrus-cured fish in coconut cream (kokoda), taro leaves slow-baked in lolo, and the ceremonial kava bowl define the table.
