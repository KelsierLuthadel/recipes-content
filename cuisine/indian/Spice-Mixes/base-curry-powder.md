# Base Curry Powder

![Base Curry Powder](resources/base-curry-powder.jpg)

*The BIR base curry powder: turmeric, paprika, cumin, coriander, fenugreek and chilli ground together.*

**Prep Time:** 5 minutes

**Makes:** about 50 g

## Overview
A balanced, all-purpose curry powder used as the foundation for most BIR-style dishes.

## Ingredients
- 2 tbsp coriander powder
- 1 tbsp cumin powder
- 1 tbsp turmeric
- 1 tbsp paprika
- 1 tsp chilli powder
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 1 tsp ginger powder

## Method
1. Combine all ingredients in a bowl.
2. Mix thoroughly until uniform.
3. Store in an airtight container.

## Notes
- Mild, balanced, and versatile.
- Forms the backbone of most curries.

## Serving
Use 1-2 tsp per curry as a base layer of flavour.

## Storage
- Store in an airtight container in a cool, dry place for up to 6 months
- Keep away from direct sunlight and moisture
