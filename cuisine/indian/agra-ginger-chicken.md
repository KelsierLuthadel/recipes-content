# Agra Ginger Chicken

![Agra Ginger Chicken](resources/agra-ginger-chicken.jpg)

*Agra's ginger chicken: bone-in chicken simmered in a deep tomato-onion gravy heavy with fresh ginger, garlic and green chilli.*

**Serves:** 4-6

**Prep Time:** 10 minutes

**Cook Time:** 1 hour

## Overview
Agra's ginger chicken is a North Indian home-cook's curry from the Taj Mahal's city: bone-in chicken simmered in a deep tomato-onion gravy heavy with fresh ginger, garlic and green chilli, finished with wilted spinach and a generous squeeze of lime. The dish leans light and cleansing rather than heavy and cream-rich, distinct from the Punjabi butter-chicken school further north; Agra cooking sits between Rajasthani spice intensity and Delhi delicacy, and this curry shows that middle ground clearly. Fresh ginger is the soul of the dish; the traditional Agra version uses three times as much ginger as a normal North Indian curry, both for the bright pungent heat and for the digestive properties the spice is supposed to bring. Bone-in chicken thighs give the proper depth; boneless cuts work but the gravy goes thinner. Eat with hot basmati or fresh chapatis, with a wedge of lime alongside.

## Ingredients
- 80 ml (2 ½ fl oz/⅓ cup) vegetable oil

### Aromatics
- 2 white onions (large), halved and thinly sliced
- 5 cm (2 inch) fresh root ginger, peeled and grated
- 6 garlic cloves, minced

### Whole spices
- 4 green cardamom pods
- 2 cinnamon sticks

### Ground spices
- 3 tbsp [Garam Masala](../../base-ingredients/curry-powder/garam-masala.md)
- ½ tsp ground turmeric
- ¼ tsp chilli powder

### Protein
- 750 g (1 lb 10 oz) bone-in chicken thighs, quartered

### Sauce
- 400 g (14 oz) canned chopped tomatoes
- 450 ml (15 fl oz/1 ¾ cups) water
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tsp sugar

### Greens and garnish
- 200 g (7 oz) baby spinach, washed
- 1 lime (zest)
- 1 small bunch fresh coriander, leaves and stalks roughly chopped

## Method

### Stage 1 - Sauté base
1. Heat oil in large heavy pan over medium-high heat.
1. Add onions, ginger and garlic; fry 8 mins until onions soften and turn deep golden brown.

### Stage 2 - Toast spices and brown chicken
1. Add garam masala, cardamom, cinnamon, turmeric and chilli powder.
1. Add chicken thighs; fry 5 mins until chicken begins to brown and is well coated.

### Stage 3 - Simmer curry
1. Add chopped tomatoes, water, salt and sugar; bring to boil.
1. Reduce heat to low; simmer gently 35-40 mins until chicken is cooked through and tender.

### Stage 4 - Finish and serve
1. Stir in baby spinach and lime zest until spinach wilts.
1. Add chopped coriander and adjust seasoning.
1. Add more water to loosen if needed before serving.

## Notes
- The onion-ginger base and gentle simmer keep the curry light.
- Use bone-in thighs for best flavor; chicken breast can be substituted with slightly less cooking time.
- Chili amount can be adjusted for heat preference.

## Serving
- Serve with steamed rice or naan.
- Garnish with extra coriander and lime wedges.

## Storage
- Refrigerate 2-3 days in airtight container.
- Reheat gently on stovetop with splash of water.
- Freeze up to 2 months; thaw overnight in fridge.
