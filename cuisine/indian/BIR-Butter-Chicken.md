# Butter Chicken

![Butter Chicken](resources/butter-chicken.png)

*The British-Indian-Restaurant butter chicken: tandoori-marinated chicken in a creamy tomato-and-butter gravy sweetened with cashew and finished with kasuri methi.*

**Serves:** 2

**Prep Time:** 10 minutes

**Cook Time:** 20 minutes

## Overview
BIR butter chicken is the restaurant adaptation of the classic Delhi murgh makhani, a creamy tomato sauce sweetened with sugar, banana and rose water, finished with butter, cream and a generous handful of fenugreek. This is the British-Indian-Restaurant version, faster than the authentic Delhi preparation: it uses pre-cooked tandoori chicken from the day's prep and the base curry gravy that every BIR kitchen keeps ready. The flavour profile is mild, rich and a touch sweet, designed to be approachable across the restaurant menu. The colour is the proper orange-pink that defines the dish; if it's brown, the cook hasn't built the tomato-and-butter base properly. Serve with naan, basmati rice and a side of mild dhal.

## Ingredients

### Chicken Marinade
- 400 g  [Pre-Cooked Chicken](Base/pre-cooked-chicken.md)
- 2 tbsp yogurt
- 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
- 1 tsp chilli powder
- 1 tsp [Garam Masala](../../base-ingredients/curry-powder/garam-masala.md)
- Salt

### Base
- 2 tbsp butter (or oil)
- 1 onion (chopped)
- 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
- 2 tbsp tomato paste
- 250ml [Curry Base Gravy](Base/curry-base.md)

### Finish
- 3 tbsp cream
- 1 tbsp butter
- 1 tsp sugar
- ½ tsp [Garam Masala](../../base-ingredients/curry-powder/garam-masala.md)

---

## Method

### Stage 1 Prep
1. Toss the pre-cooked chicken in the marinade (yogurt, ginger-garlic, chilli powder, garam masala, salt); rest 15 minutes while you build the base. Skip this step if your pre-cooked chicken was already tandoori-marinated.

### Stage 2 Build Base
1. Heat butter/oil.
1. Cook onions with salt until soft.
1. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook 1 minute.
1. Add tomato paste and cook until darkened.

### Stage 3 Cook
1. Add base gravy and chicken.
1. Simmer 10-15 minutes.

### Stage 4 Finish
1. Add cream, butter, sugar, garam masala.
1. Simmer until rich and smooth.

---

## Notes
- Should be mild, creamy, slightly sweet

## Storage
- Refrigerate 2-3 days in airtight container
- Freeze up to 2 months; thaw fully before reheating
- Reheat gently on low heat with a splash of stock or water
- Best eaten within 24 hours for peak flavour
