# Cuban Cuisine

![Cuban Cuisine](resources/hero.jpg)

Cuba on a plate, where Spanish, African and Caribbean strands are woven together with rice, beans, pork and citrus. Sofrito (onion, garlic, pepper, cumin), oregano and bay set the base, while sour-orange mojo brightens roasted and braised meats. Long gentle braising sits alongside griddled and fried preparations.
