# French Cuisine

![French Cuisine](resources/hero.jpg)

The grammar of Western cooking: refined regional and bistro traditions built on butter, cream, herbs and stocks. Shallot, mustard, wine and brandy do much of the seasoning, with mother sauces and reductions giving every dish its finish. Slow braises (boeuf bourguignon, coq au vin), pan sauces and patisserie technique anchor the canon.
