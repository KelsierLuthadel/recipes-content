# Paraguayan Cuisine

![Paraguayan Cuisine](resources/hero.jpg)

Paraguay's corn-and-cassava table: sopa paraguaya (which despite the name is a solid corn-and-cheese cake), chipa cheese-bread that turns up at every bus station, mbeju cassava pancakes, asado paraguayo at family gatherings, and a national obsession with terere (the cold mate). Guarani-and-Spanish food culture; cheese, corn and beef define the plate.
