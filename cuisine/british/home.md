# British Cuisine

![British Cuisine](resources/hero.jpg)

Home cooking from the British Isles: Sunday roasts, slow-braised stews, baked puddings and frugal one-pots from leftovers. Worcestershire, mustard, mint, sage and thyme handle the seasoning; flour, butter, beef stock and dripping build pies and gravies. Defining techniques include the Sunday roast with all the trimmings, the steamed and baked pudding, the suet-crust pie and the dripping-fried chip.
