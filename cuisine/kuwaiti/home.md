# Kuwaiti Cuisine

![Kuwaiti Cuisine](resources/hero.jpg)

A Persian Gulf crossroads cuisine shaped by Bedouin desert cooking, Indian Ocean trade routes and the pearl-diving coast. Dried lime (loomi) and saffron set the palette; long-grain basmati cooks with spice-marinated chicken, lamb or Gulf shrimp into machboos, the daily centrepiece. Cardamom, baharat, turmeric and rose water carry the perfume; dates and fresh dairy from the desert pantry close the meal. Ramadan iftar anchors the year's cooking, with harees, balaleet and luqaimat returning to every table.
