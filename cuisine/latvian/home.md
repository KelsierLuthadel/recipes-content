# Latvian Cuisine

![Latvian Cuisine](resources/hero.jpg)

A rye-bread country: Latvians say "where there is rye, there is Latvia" and the dark sourdough rupjmaize sits on every table from breakfast to supper. The Christmas centrepiece is pelēkie zirņi ar speķi (grey peas with bacon), the festive salad is rasols (potato and herring), and Baltic herring threads through the year smoked, salted and pickled. Riga's Hanseatic-German past shaped the pork chop and the rye-bread pudding, while the forests give chanterelles, lingonberries and birch sap, all bottled, dried and frozen for the long winter.
