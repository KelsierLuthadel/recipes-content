# Texas Cuisine

![Texas Cuisine](resources/hero.jpg)

Texan cooking: the meeting of cowboy chuckwagon, German and Czech immigrant baking, Mexican border food and Southern hospitality. Brisket smoked for fourteen hours over post oak in Central Texas, chile con queso and chicken-fried steak at every Sunday supper, frito pie at the football, Tex-Mex enchiladas with chile gravy, kolaches from the Czech bakeries, Texas sheet cake at every bake sale, and chilli con carne (the state dish, without beans) at every roadhouse.
