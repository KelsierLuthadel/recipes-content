# Saint Lucian Cuisine

![Saint Lucian Cuisine](resources/hero.jpg)

An Eastern Caribbean cuisine layered with French and English colonial inheritance, African slow-cooking and the island's own creole interpretation. Green-fig-and-saltfish (the national dish), island bouyon stew, conch in creole sauce, fragrant cocoa-tea simmered with cinnamon stick, and the rum punch poured at the end of every meal define the table.
