# Southern Cuisine

![Southern Cuisine](resources/hero.jpg)

The cooking of the American South, built on West African, Native American, Scotch-Irish and French Caribbean foundations and shaped by three hundred years of farm-and-river-and-coast geography. Buttermilk-brined Southern fried chicken, slow-smoked pulled pork barbecue, low-country boil on butcher paper, shrimp and grits, Hoppin' John with black-eyed peas on New Year's Day, chicken and dumplings on a cold night, country ham with red-eye gravy at breakfast. Collard greens cooked low and slow with smoked pork, sweet potato casserole at Thanksgiving, macaroni and cheese as a vegetable. Banana pudding, peach cobbler, sweet potato pie, pimento cheese, deviled eggs and hot pepper jelly cover the rest of the table.
