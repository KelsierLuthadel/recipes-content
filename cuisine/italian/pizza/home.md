# Pizza

## Overview

Neapolitan and home-style pizzas, with high-hydration doughs slow-fermented for flavour and structure. Toppings stay restrained: san marzano tomato, fior di latte or fresh mozzarella, basil, olive oil and a few well-chosen extras. Whether wood-fired or baked at home, the goal is a charred, airy crust under fresh, simple toppings.
